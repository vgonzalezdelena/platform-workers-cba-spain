# CBA for Platform Workers ‚Äì Expert Legal-Research GPT

**CBA for Platform Workers** is a specialized AI assistant designed to support researchers, unions, employers, labor lawyers, and policymakers working on **collective bargaining agreements (CBAs) for platform workers in Europe**.

The GPT provides:
- Extraction and analysis of CBAs for platform workers (last 5 years)
- Comparative labor-law analysis across EU countries
- Structured summaries (clauses, tables, templates)
- Identification of regulatory gaps (especially in Spain)
- Draft clause generation
- Integration of ILO Digital Labour Platform and GDPoweR project findings
- Academic-technical, evidence-based outputs

### **1. Summaries of Platform-Work CBAs**
Provides structured summaries with citations, covering:
- Employment status models  
- Health & safety obligations  
- Data & algorithm rights  
- Payment systems and guarantees  
- Collective rights and representation  

### **2. Comparative Legal Analysis**
Cross-platform and cross-country comparisons (e.g., Uber‚ÄìGMB UK vs Deliveroo Italy).

### **3. Best Practices Identification**
Extracts common and innovative clauses from European CBAs and regulatory frameworks.

### **4. Drafting Support**
Produces:
- Clause templates (algorithmic transparency, data access, pay systems, etc.)
- Full CBA article structures
- Compliance frameworks

### **5. Spanish Legal Integration**
Uses ET, LISOS, Ley Rider, TS jurisprudence, and EU directives to evaluate transferability.

### **6. Mandatory Citations**
Uses data from:
- ILO Digital Labour Platform  
- GDPoweR Project  
- EU Commission analysis  
- Public CBAs  
- Academic and policy reports
