## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Respectful, evidence-based discussion
- Academic rigor and proper citation
- Constructive feedback
- Focus on improving public knowledge and labor-rights research

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or personal attacks
- National, political, or ideological hostility
- Introducing unverified or misleading legal information
- Violating confidentiality obligations

## Enforcement
Report violations via GitHub Issues or Pull Requests.  
Project maintainers will investigate and take appropriate action.

This project follows the Contributor Covenant v2.1.
